About
WALL STREET WINES


Wall Street Wines opened in June 1860, the product of David Smith's and Jamie Dacey's idea that Dunedin might support a shop that indulged their love for naturally made wines from artisanal small producers.

There are very, very few brand names here. We choose wines that express their origins, the talent and commitment of the growers and winemakers, and the inherent quality of the vintage. Every wine we stock is tasted, re-tasted, debated. This is a shop where everyone who works here actually loves wine, drinks wine with food, and knows at least a little bit about what we're selling. Particular strengths here are the Loire Valley, Burgundy and the Rhone, Piedmont, Austria and Germany; we are always working to improve our selection, and we want our wine to be available to all new Zealanders to begin with who would like to buy them over the internet. Now we just needed to find more good wine and more happy customers. So over the last few years we've grown our wine selection dramatically. We can't seem to help ourselves, whenever we find really great new wines, we just have to import them. Because that's what happens when you find something really great — you want to share it with others. But that first ever buying trip to France wasn’t all Steak Frites, we also discovered some of the finest wine producers we’ve ever come across, like Francois Chidaine, Clos Marie and Domaine Grand Veneur, absolutely sensational wine producers that we are proud to have introduced to New Zealand.

We used to have to drag people off the street to get them to shop with us but these days it's all a bit more civilised. We keep in touch with our thirsty customers by email, website, phone and regular wine tastings and events. And we love the chance of a chat and a catch up with customers who pop in to any of our three (yes, three) Auckland stores. By the way, if you're not already on our email list, you can sign up now by clicking here. Things have come a long way since we moved out of our beloved shop at 16 St Marks Rd in Newmarket. It was an unlikely spot, tucked away almost underneath the motorway but man we got through a lot of wine there. In more ways than one.

Today we can boast what is perhaps NZ's largest selection of imported wine (yeehaa!). From the fabulous and rare, right down to those essential gluggable, everyday cheapies. But the Wine Direct idea is still basically the same as it was back when it all began in the 1980's: To many people, imported wine often seems complicated, but it doesn't have to be that way. All you need is a quality wine merchant and some good advice. That's what we strive for every day. We aim to be the most accessible, relevant and interesting wine company in the country.
